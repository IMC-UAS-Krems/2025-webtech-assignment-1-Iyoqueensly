I Love Flowers

My name is Iyobosa Queensly Osayantin, known as Iyo. I was born in Benin City, Nigeria in the year 1999. As a seven year old, I moved to Austria, and at the age of 23, I realised that I loved creating bouquets. I started by just putting few pieces of tulips together until I discovered the rest. Ever since, I've created various types of boquets for different types of occasions,which you are welcome to check out in "My Work". I am currently a student and create bouquets for fun.

I do not have many hobbies, I just love reading books, preferably psychological or philosophical ones. Music makes me extremly happy, expecially classical ones, so I listen to them a lot. I don't mind other genres either, as long as it sounds good, I will add it to my playlists. Not many people like studying, but I do. Learning about new things excite me no matter how difficult they get, I am quite headstrong and believe I can do anything I put my mind to. Which is why I tend to open up a flower shop when I retire from the tech industry. Hopefully by then, I get to learn how to make the most beautiful bouquets and put smiles on peoples faces and add some of natures beauty to their spaces. My absolute favourite thing to do is give people presents. It is delightful to see them happy because of me.

This website was created to share my passion with you. To show you that flowers make life better. I hope you have as much fun looking through it as much as I had creating it.
